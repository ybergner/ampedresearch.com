Our research lab, Analytical Methods for Progressive Educational Design (AMPED), is dedicated to developing methodology to fill gaps in learning and assessment design. The application areas we focus on for methodology are collaboration, problem-solving processes, and project-oriented learning spaces such as Fab Labs and Makerspaces.
Our work bridges three disciplines: learning analytics, with its expansive uses of data and technology to optimize outcomes; psychometrics, with its focus on precision in the construction of psychological measurement models; and curriculum design, which should ideally concur with assessment design.
